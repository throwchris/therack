ABOUT THE RACK

The Rack on 72 was founded by Mrs. Smith from Stillman Valley. Always wanting to have her own establishment, she found a building off of 72 not too far from Stillman Creek. Renovations began and it soon became the towns go to well.

We believe nothing goes better than a nice juicy quarter pound burger off the grill and your favorite ice cold beer!
